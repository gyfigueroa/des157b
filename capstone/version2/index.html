What's the big idea?

It’s got something to do with gorillas. Bloomberg’s Hannah Fry discussed the gorilla problem in depth here .


Basically, the gorilla problem is a concept popularized by Artifical Intelligence (AI) researcher Stuart Russel of whether humans can maintain their supremacy and autonomy in a world that includes machines with substantially greater intelligence.

So… what actually makes an AI intelligent?

1

Ability to learn and adapt

2

Ability to reason (requires a conceptual understanding of the world)

3

Interacts with environment to achieve goals
